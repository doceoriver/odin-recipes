Gochujang Butter Noodles

Description:

A quick, delicious and spicy recipe perfect for a cozy night in!

Ingredients:

  • 7 ounces of long noodles
  • 4 tablespoons of salted butter
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/4 tablespoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang paste
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1/4 cup of pasta water
  • 1 chopped green onion

Preparation:

  1. In a pan over medium low heat, melt 3 tbsp butter, add garlic and saut√© the minced garlic for 30 seconds.
  2. Add in the salt, pepper, gochujang, honey, and rice wine vinegar.
  3. Cook for 3 mins over medium low heat.
  4. Add in the al dente pasta along with 1 tbsp of butter and the pasta water.
  5. Cook and combine for 3-5 mins or until the starch thickens a bit and the pasta absorbs the sauce.
  6. Serve with green onions and enjoy!
